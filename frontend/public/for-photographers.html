FAQ

Common questions

Is it really free to join SnapBook as a photographer? ▾
Yes — completely free. There is no registration fee, no monthly subscription, and no hidden charges. SnapBook only earns when you earn: we take a 10% platform fee from each completed booking. Everything else goes to you.
How does payment work? ▾
Customers pay upfront when they place a booking. The payment is held securely by SnapBook and released to your account once the booking is marked as completed. You receive 90% of the total booking amount.
Can I set my own rates and packages? ▾
Absolutely. You control your hourly rate and can create up to three packages (Basic, Standard, Premium) each with custom prices, durations, and included features. You can update them any time from your dashboard.
What if a customer cancels last-minute? ▾
SnapBook has a built-in dispute resolution system. Late cancellations are governed by our cancellation policy and you are protected. You can also open a dispute if you believe a cancellation was unfair — our admin team reviews every case.
Do I need professional equipment to join? ▾
There is no equipment requirement to sign up. However, photographers who upload high-quality portfolio images and maintain strong reviews naturally attract more bookings. We recommend showcasing your best work from the start.
How long does profile approval take? ▾
Once you submit your profile, our admin team reviews it within 24 hours. You'll get a notification once you're approved and your profile goes live on the platform for customers to discover.
